The Problem

Three things that
keep people out.

💸
Gear costs too much

A decent entry-level controller starts at $300. Professional setups run thousands. Open Decks runs in your browser. The gear question goes away entirely.

📚
No one explains the rules

Most DJ education is YouTube chaos or $500 courses. We wrote The Rulebook: a practitioner's field guide to the craft. Free. Written by working DJs.

🚪
The scene is closed

DJ culture has gatekeeping problems. Open Decks doesn't lower the bar — it removes the invisible walls. The skills are real. The access should be too.

The Technology

Your library.
Our intelligence.

Open Decks connects to Spotify, Apple Music, or local files. It analyzes BPM, harmonic key, era, energy, and lyrical content — then turns that data into real challenges. The Alphabet Run navigates your actual library. Phrase Finder finds emotional bridges between tracks. Camelot Wild Mode builds harmonic instinct under pressure.

Who This Is For

Early Stage

Educator / Practitioner

The Steward track. Governance role in Open X. Shape the curriculum. Contribute what no API contains.

"The closed booth, the closed circle, the closed scene — those days are over."

Open Decks is a program of Open X — a Richmond, VA nonprofit that believes music culture belongs to everyone. Our Stewards are established DJs who don't just teach. They govern. Every tool in this platform was shaped by practitioners, not developers.
