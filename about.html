About Open Decks

A platform built
by a movement.

Open Decks is the first program of Open X — a Richmond, VA nonprofit building platforms that democratize music performance culture. We're not a startup. We're a community governed by the people who built the culture we're opening up.

The Parent Organisation

Open X

● Open X · 501(c)(3)

What is Open X?

Open X is a nonprofit organisation founded on a single belief: music culture transmits through people, not gatekeepers. We build programs that invite people into music performance culture — not as spectators, but as participants.

Open Decks is the first program. It starts with DJing because DJing is one of the most technically inaccessible performance skills in music — and one of the most culturally rich. Expensive gear. Scene gatekeeping. A craft learned by proximity rather than by design. We're changing that.

01
Open by Design

No credential required to start. No gear required. No scene membership. The platform meets you where you are — with sample playlists if you have nothing, or with your full library if you've been collecting for years.

02
Community Governed

Open X uses sociocratic governance. Stewards hold real authority — not advisory roles. They consent on curriculum changes, contribute cultural knowledge, and hold seats in the organizational structure.

03
Education First

Every tool teaches something true about DJing. The Rulebook and Rule-Break Guide are the intellectual core. The challenges are applied practice. The Steward commentary is the master class.

The Collective

Stewards don't advise.
They govern.

"You helped build this culture. Help us make sure it doesn't close ranks on the next generation. You have a seat at the table — not a plaque on the wall."

Stewards are established DJs and performers who hold real authority in Open X's governance structure. They are not volunteers. They are not advisors. They are co-owners of a cultural institution — with the ability to block decisions that violate the mission.

What Stewards do:

Consent on curriculum and tool design changes
Post weekly community Challenge Mode Gig Briefs
Contribute scene knowledge that no API contains
Hold linked roles in Open X's General Circle
Annotate The Rulebook and Rule-Break Guide
Provide debrief commentary for Architect-tier players

Richmond, VA · Chapter Zero

Where it starts.

Open Decks launches in Richmond. We're building with partners who've been building the scene for years. Richmond isn't the test market — it's the foundation. Open Decks goes wherever communities invite it.

Anchor Partner
Loso

Richmond's destination for underground dance music. World-class sound, safe space policy, W. Broad St.

Jazz & Open Jam
Révéler

Cabaret and curiosities in Carytown. Weekly open jam sessions — players of all levels share the stage.

Community Partner
Common House

Social club in the Arts District with podcast studio, event halls, and community programming.

Event Partner
The Broadberry

Beloved mid-size concert room on W. Broad. Decades of commitment to Richmond's music scene.

Cultural Partner
The Hippodrome

Jackson Ward's historic theater, rooted in Black performance culture. Our equity anchor.

Arts Partner
Gallery5

Cross-disciplinary art and performance space on N. 5th. The spirit of Open Decks in architecture.

Showcase Partner
The Camel

All genres, all people. Richmond's most eclectic stage — from punk to electronic to bluegrass.
